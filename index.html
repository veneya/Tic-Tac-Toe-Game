RULES:

  • Players:

    Two players take turns.


  • Game Setup:

    Played on a 3x3 grid with 9 squares.


  • How to Play:

    Player 1 (X) starts, then players alternate turns.


  • Winning:

    Get three marks in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal).


  • Draw:

    If no winner and grid is full, it's a draw.


  • Reset:

    Reset the board after each game.
